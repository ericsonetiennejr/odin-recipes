Instant Pot Korean Beef

Description

This savory instant pot dish is an amazing mixture of convenience and taste. The most intensive part of this dish is waiting for it to cook inside of the instant pot!

Ingredients

    Sauce

  • ½cup reduced-sodium sauce
  • ⅓ cup packed brown sugar
  • ¼ cup reduced-sodium beef broth
  • 5 minced gloves of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons freshly grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoons gochujang sauce
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon pepper

    • Meat

  • 3-4 pounds boneless beef chuck roast cut into 1-inch cubes

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together all of the sauce ingredients.
  2. Place the cubed meat into your Instant Pot.
  3. Pour the sauce into the Instant Pot.
  4. Close your instant pot, ensure that the pressure release valve is turned to SEALING.
  5. Press the MEAT program button. Set the time to 40 minutes. Start.
  6. After the pot has stopped the program(usually indicated by beeping and it switching to KEEP WARM mode), let it release pressure for 25 minutes.
  7. After that time, release any remaining pressure by turning the pressure release valve to VENTING.
  8. ENJOY.

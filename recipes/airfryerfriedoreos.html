Air Fryer Fried Oreos

Description

The absoulte peak of comfort food, unlikely to help you reach any health goals...though the dopamine boost may keep you going for a bit more.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of Pillsbury Crescent Rolls
  • 8 Oreos

Instructions

  1. Find a clean surface.
  2. Unroll the crescent roll dough onto said clean surface.
  3. Cover one Oreo at a time with the crescent roll dough, collect the excess to potentially use it on another cookie.
  4. After all cookies are covered: Preheat your air fryer to 320F. If your air fryer does not allow for specific temperature controls, put it on air fryer mode.
  5. Place the cookies inside of the air fryer if it has a basket, and ensure that they do not touch. If you lack a basket, but have a tray: Oil a large enough section of parchment paper, and place the cookies on that prior to placing them on the tray.
  6. Allow them to cook until they are golden brown on the outside.
  7. Remove the cookies, and allow them to cool for 3 minutes.
